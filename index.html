The Front Door

Two quick routines. The key always turns clockwise.

How to enter

Arriving
1
Turn the key a full turn — 360° clockwise.
2
Push the handle all the way down.
3
Turn the key again to open.
4
Once inside, please remember to lock the door.

How to lock

Indoors
1
Close the door.
2
Lift the handle up 100%.
3
Turn the key a full turn — 360° clockwise.
4
Check the handle
Is the door locked?
